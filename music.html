Music of Lion Dance

Drums 鼓

The Drum represents the Lions' roar and its emotion. When there is one lion performing, the drum follows the lead of the lion, but if there is more than one lion, the lions follow the drummer.

Cymbals 鈸

To support the drum, there are usually 2 or more cymbols in a performance.

Gong 鑼

To support the drum. One gong per performance. When recording the performance in video, the gong is what sounds the loudest.
